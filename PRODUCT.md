# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Locksmith shop owners and small teams (solo operators to crews of 2-5) running day-to-day operations from a shop counter or mobile device. Multi-tenant SaaS: many different locksmith businesses each have their own instance. Some users are older and not highly tech-savvy; the system must feel approachable without dumbing down functionality. Primary tasks are quick: log a service, create a quote, check inventory, register a sale.

## Product Purpose

A multi-tenant SaaS business management system for locksmith shops in Spanish-speaking markets. Covers the full operational loop: customer records, service catalog, quotations, sales and invoicing, inventory, warranty tracking, and tools management. A locksmith owner should be able to run their entire business from this one tool. Success means zero friction at the counter; the shop owner reaches for this instead of a notebook or a spreadsheet.

## Brand Personality

Modern, fast, tech-forward. Precise and purposeful, like a well-made tool. The product should feel like something a professional chose because it's fast and trustworthy, not because it was cheap or easy to set up. Reference energy: Linear (dense, keyboard-friendly, dark-mode-native, no wasted chrome).

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS white/blue/rounded corporate look (the "startup template" aesthetic)
- Dated desktop-software feel (Windows XP-era tables and modal stacks)
- Consumer-grade friendliness (overly rounded, emoji-heavy, hand-holdy)
- Complex enterprise UI (three-panel layouts, icon-only sidebars with 20 items, overwhelming dashboards)
- POS system aesthetics (garish colors, oversized buttons, receipt-printer legacy)

## Design Principles

1. **Speed is a feature.** Every interaction should feel instant. No loading spinners for things that can optimistically update; no confirmation dialogs for reversible actions.
2. **Dense by design.** A locksmith glancing at the screen mid-job needs information at a glance. Show more, scroll less. Tables over cards where data density matters.
3. **Trust through precision.** Clean structure, consistent patterns, and predictable behavior inspire confidence. This is a business tool handling real money and customer records.
4. **Counter-ready.** The app is used at a shop counter and on a phone, not just a quiet office desk. Touch targets must be generous; critical actions must be reachable with one hand.
5. **Accessible without being obvious.** Older and non-tech-savvy users should never feel lost. Clear labels, logical hierarchy, and forgiving interactions, without making the product feel childish or slow for power users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Special attention to: generous tap targets (min 44px), clear and descriptive labels on all form controls, sufficient color contrast in both light and dark themes, readable font sizes at default zoom. No reliance on color alone to communicate state.
